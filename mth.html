MT Højgaard

Various Film, Photography & Post-productions

What I did?
During the second year of my masters degree I started working at MT Højgaard. Here I was part of a two man team with the goal to create content that would help the company win new building projects. I took part in the creative direction, I created storyboards, arranged and conducted interviews, searched for insights, filmed, edited, sound-designed and photographed.

What I learned?
I experienced how svisual storytelling can be used to communicate complex thematics in a digestible way. I found that taking the users concerns into account can be a great entry for designing. I learned the mechanics of a camera and how to do 360 degrees post-production on film.
