Deadline Syria

A documentary driven e-learning game

What is it?
Deadline Syria is an interactive e-learning game, where the player takes the role of a journalist visiting asylum camps in Denmark and Jordan. Your task is to conduct interviews and find clues about the lives of Syrian refugee kids currently living in the asylum camps. The game is designed for the thematic journal-week that is part of our curriculum in danish schools. When the students have collected enough clues they are told to write a story about the individuals they encountered in the game. The game is build in a video driven web tool called Klynt.

What I did?
My tasks was to transcribe interviews, consult and re-create the wireframes, build the user-interface, cut and grade video material, make it go live and do versioning.
